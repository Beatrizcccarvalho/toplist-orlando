About Our BBQ Grill Rental Service

Make your Orlando vacation even more memorable with our premium BBQ grill rental. Whether you're staying at a vacation home with a private pool area or at a resort with outdoor space, our BBQ grills are the perfect addition to your stay.

Our professional team will deliver, set up, and collect the grill at your convenience. All grills are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized between rentals, ensuring you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

What's Included:

  • High-quality propane grill
  • Full propane tank (additional tank available on request)
  • Grill tools set (spatula, tongs, fork)
  • Cleaning brush
  • Drip tray liners
  • Setup and collection
