About This Resort

Fantasy World Resort offers the perfect combination of convenience, comfort, and fun for your Orlando vacation. The resort features a water park with slides, a lazy river, and multiple swimming pools. Guests can enjoy the fitness center, tennis courts, and on-site restaurant.

Located just 3 miles from Disney World, this gated community provides a secure and enjoyable environment for your family. All vacation homes in this resort offer modern amenities and are professionally maintained.
